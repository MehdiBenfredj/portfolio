🤔 Why this exists

Two reasons. One: I needed somewhere to think out loud that wasn't owned by a company that might decide tomorrow it doesn't want me anymore.

Two: I missed the old web. The one made by hand. The one that linked sideways instead of just up. So I built one.

It's plain HTML, plain CSS, and a sprinkle of JS. No framework. No build. The source is over here — fork it, rip it apart, make your own.
