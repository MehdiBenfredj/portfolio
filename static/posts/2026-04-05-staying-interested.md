Curiosity isn't a feeling, it's a discipline. Some practices that have worked for me, and some that haven't.

## Worked

- **Reading outside the field.** A novel a month makes the rest of my reading better.
- **Writing badly in public.** Lowering the bar to publish is the single biggest thing.
- **Following weird people.** The interesting ones aren't optimized for the algorithm.

## Didn't work

- "Productivity systems." All of them. Every one. They are a coping mechanism, not a tool.
- Trying to follow ten things at once. Two is the limit.
- Forcing it. Real curiosity has a smell. So does fake curiosity.
